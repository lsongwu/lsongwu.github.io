Feast @ Johansson Projects

11/2025 - 01/2026

Feast is structured like an Italian full-course meal, unfolding in stages that explore the eroticism, performance, and violence behind acts of eating and consumption. Each painting resembles a thumbnail for a mukbang video —marked by surreal excess, seductive abundance, and intimate spectacle.

Elsewhere @ IRL Gallery

11/2024 - 12/2024

"Wu’s paintings unsettle like a phone camera one didn’t expect to be front-facing [...] Elsewhere turns the urgency to look into a humiliation ritual." - Ekalan Hou

Out in the Open @ New Image Art

12/2023 - 01/2024

Out in the Open is a body of four paintings that collapses a decadent seafood mukbang, impossible skydivers, unsettling voyeurs, and a cinematic frame into a single viewable room. Weaving between enclosed and open spaces, the figures in Wu’s work feel impossible and impenetrable, elusive in both their singularity and doubleness, nakedness and exposure.

L. Song Wu is an artist currently dividing her time between northern California and Milan. Her work explores the tension between intimacy and alienation through the lens of surveillance/viewership. Drawing from a range of sources like anime, YouTube thumbnails, and memory, Wu creates a meticulously crafted world that refracts her ideas of place and self. Wu's work has been exhibited internationall, and she has been selected as a finalist for the AXA Art Prize and Tournesol Award.
