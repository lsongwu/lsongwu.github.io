Bio

L. Song Wu is a figurative painter originally from Tampa, Florida, currently residing and working in northern California. Her paintings explore the tension between intimacy and alienation, challenging viewers to confront ther own sense of belonging and the constructions of the female figure. Drawing inspiration from anime, YouTube thumbnails, and her own personal archive, Wu's meticulously crafted world is both inviting and oppressing. Wu's work has been exhibited in New York, Paris, Los Angeles, and San Francisco. She has been selected as a finalist for the AXA Art Prize and the Tournesol Award.
